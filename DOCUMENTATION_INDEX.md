## â“ FAQ

### Q: How often should I publish new articles?

**A:** Start with 1 per week for the first month, then adjust based on analytics. Weekly publishing is ideal for SEO growth.

### Q: How do I track blog performance?

**A:** See metrics to track in **BLOG_SHOWCASE.md** â†’ "Success Metrics to Track"

### Q: Can I change article categories?

**A:** Yes, update the `category` field in blogs.js and add filter button to blogs.html if needed.

### Q: How do I promote articles?

**A:** See "Pro Tips for Success" in **BLOG_QUICK_REFERENCE.md**

### Q: What's the best image size?

**A:** 900x500px works well with responsive CSS. Unsplash provides high-quality free images.

### Q: Can I edit existing articles?

**A:** Yes, just update the `content` field in the corresponding object in blogs.js and refresh the page.
